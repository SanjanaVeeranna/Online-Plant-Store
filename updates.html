----UPDATES OF COMPANY----
PRONIA is taking significant strides to enhance its support for individuals with early-onset dementia and their caregivers with
a new program, ‘Stronger Together’ that kicked off during a special event held at the Treacy Center in Parkville last Friday. We are
trying to do something new and more significant on the topic of dementia because we are aware that there is a bigger issue
in our community till today of date.

With a gathering of over one hundred guests, CEO Tina Douvos-Stathopoulou expressed her gratitude to
the Commonwealth Department of Health and Aged Care for funding PRONIA to develop a culturally
responsive and secure care program for people with early dementia and their caregivers.
“Stronger Together will not only elevate our dementia care initiatives but also extend
our services to metropolitan Melbourne and Tasmania,”
Mrs Douvos-Stathopoulou said.
